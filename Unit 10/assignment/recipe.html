My Favorite Recipe: Gordon Ramsay's Eggy Bread w/ Sugared Apples

Ingredient 1 Ingredient 2 Ingredient 3 Ingredient 4 Ingredient 5 Ingredient 6

4 Apples
Bread
5 Tbsp Sugar
Caramel Sauce
3 Eggs
4 Tbsp Milk
Instructions:
For the sliced apples (used as a topping), add caramel sauce and 3 tbsp sugar.
For the bread, dump in mixture of 3 eggs, 4 tbsp milk, 2 tbsp sugar, and add to hot pan until brown on both sides.
