Why You Should Have Oat Milk Over Regular Milk

Alongside the fact that it simply tastes better,
Oat milk is a superior alternative to regular milk for these top 3 reasons:

  1. Better for the environment

  2. Cuts down on the waste of water

  3. Non-discriminatory to those who are lactose-intolerant (it's inclusive!)
